Our Story

About Trinity

The Beginning

A Vision Rooted in Passion

Trinity Restaurant was born from a simple yet powerful idea — that food is the universal language of love, culture, and community. What began as a dream shared between three friends over a modest dinner has grown into one of Hesaraghatta's most cherished dining destinations.

We believe that a great meal is not just about the food on the plate. It's about the story behind it, the hands that crafted it, the ambience that surrounds it, and the people you share it with. Every dish at Trinity is a chapter in a greater narrative — one that celebrates the diverse culinary heritage of India and the world.

Situated in the tranquil greenery of Hesaraghatta, Bengaluru, Trinity Restaurant offers an escape from the ordinary. Our four unique dining spaces cater to every mood — from intimate celebrations to grand family gatherings — each designed to provide a deeply personal and unforgettable experience.

Every Hall, a Different World

01
Luxury · Fine Dining
The Skyrane Hall

An opulent sanctuary of luxury dining — The Skyrane Hall enchants guests with soaring ceilings adorned with hand-crafted crystal chandeliers, curated art installations, and plush seating upholstered in silk-blend fabrics.

Capacity: 80 Guests Private Dining Available Live Classical Music Curated Wine Pairing
02
Family · Open Air
Desire Courtyard

The Desire Courtyard is an open-air haven, framed by lush tropical plants, cascading water features, and warm Edison string lights. Designed with families in mind, a relaxed atmosphere where conversations flow as naturally as the food.

Capacity: 150 Guests Kids' Play Zone Semi-Open Air Event Hosting
03
Casual · Quick Bites
Snack Arena

Fast, bold, and unapologetically fun — the Snack Arena is Trinity's tribute to the vibrant street food culture of India. With interactive open kitchen counters, neon-lit boards, and a buzzing ambiance.

No Reservation Needed Open Kitchen Snack Combos Quick Service
04
Rooftop · Sunset Dining
Trinity Sky Lounge

The crown jewel of Trinity — the Sky Lounge sits atop the building, offering sweeping views of the Hesaraghatta skyline and the Deccan plateau at dusk. Dine under an open canopy of stars with handcrafted cocktails.
